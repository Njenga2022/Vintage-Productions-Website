Mixing & Mastering

- Here at Vintage Productions we always ensure that our clients get high quality mixes for their songs.
- We ensure that we always deliver on the 3D sound that is required in the modern day mixes. A common question we get is, "What is decribed as a well mixed 3D record, in the modern day production?"

Solid Low End
The mix is meant to have tight bass to complement the feeling of the track
Well Spread Mid Range
Since most of the tracks information is contained in the mid range, every instrument and drum sound needs to have its own space in the mids
An airy High End
The highs are meant to give clarity and air to the mix, therefore it should be clear and well mixed.

- All these we ensure in our mixes and so much more that haven't been mentioned above.
- If you'd wish for your track to be professionally mixed and mastered, visit our contact us page to book a session with us.
